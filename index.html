Field Operations Guide

  • Setup: Open link online ➔ menu options ➔ "Add to Home Screen". Run offline from the home screen icon.
  • Logging Assets: Enter Unit # and Level. Select an orientation (N, E, S, W, Floor) and tap 📷 Take Photo.
  • Saving Names: Press and hold (long-press) the generated image preview to open the menu and choose your target storage location.
  • Advance Level: Tap "Level Photos Finished" to increment the level sequence and reset the internal counter.
  • Close Unit: Tap "Finished Unit" to clear out all data caches for the next feature layout deployment.
