Field Operations Guide

  • Setup: Open link online ➔ menu options ➔ "Add to Home Screen". Run offline from the home screen icon.
  • Filename format: [UNIT]_[LYR]_lvl[NN]_[context]_[NN].jpg
  • Logging Photos: Enter Unit #, Layer, and Level. Select an orientation (N, E, S, W, Floor) and tap 📷 Take Photo.
  • Saving: Tap 💾 Save Photo to save with the correct filename via the share sheet.
  • Advance Level: Tap "Level Photos Finished" to increment the level and reset the photo counter.
  • Close Unit: Tap "Finished Unit" to clear all fields for the next unit.
